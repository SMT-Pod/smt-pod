        <p><strong>Bio:</strong> Tan Nazare (they/them) is an independent researcher in music theory based in California. Tan has presented their research at AMS/SMT 2025, Mechademia 2023, CUNY GSIM 2023. Tan is also an elementary school music teacher at the Community School of Music and Arts and maintains a singing private studio. They hold a Master of Music in music theory from McGill University, a Postgraduate Certificate in Vocal Pedagogy from the University of Essex, and a Bachelor in Music from the University of Edinburgh, UK.</p>
