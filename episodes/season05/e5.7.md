        <p><strong>Bio:</strong> Hang Ki Choi is pursuing a Ph.D. in music theory at the CUNY Graduate Center. She holds a M.M. degree in music theory from the Mannes School of Music and a B.S. degree in Applied Psychology from New York University. She is interested in how musical expectation and tension impact our perception of closure. Hang Ki has presented at the 2024 SMT with Joseph Straus and participated as a panelist at the 2025 AMS/SMT concert on African Pianism. At Mannes, she was awarded the Felix Salzer Techniques of Music Award for academic achievements. She currently teaches at Queens College.</p>
